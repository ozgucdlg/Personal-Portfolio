About Me

Full Stack Developer & Former Mechanical Engineer

I am a dedicated Full Stack Developer with two years of experience in designing and building scalable, high-performance applications. My expertise spans both frontend and backend development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Angular, React), Java, C#, Node.js, Spring Boot, .NET Framework, and microservices architecture.

After moving to Canada six years ago, I successfully transitioned from mechanical engineering to pursue my passion for technology. I graduated from Lambton College in 2021, marking a significant milestone in my career shift to software development.

Beyond my professional life, I maintain an active lifestyle through regular gym sessions and enjoy socializing with friends. My love for travel and photography allows me to explore and capture the essence of different cultures and landscapes, making me an adventurous and open-minded individual.
