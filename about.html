Tell me about Yourself

Ozguc Dalga is a dedicated Full Stack Developer with four years of experience in designing and building scalable, high-performance applications using modern technologies. His expertise spans both frontend and backend development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Angular, React), Java, C#, Node.js, Spring Boot, .NET Framework, and microservices architecture. Ozguc Dalga is a dedicated software developer with a background in mechanical engineering. After moving to Canada six years ago, he pursued his passion for technology and successfully transitioned into software development. He has a keen interest in strategy games and is an avid chess player, constantly challenging himself to think critically and improve his skills. In his free time, he loves to explore new places, capturing the essence of his travels through photography. His curiosity and enthusiasm discovering different cultures and landscapes make him an adventurous and open-minded individual.
